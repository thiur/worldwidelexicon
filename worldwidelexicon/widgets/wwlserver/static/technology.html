Technology

O'Reilly & Associates

O'Reilly is the leading publisher of technology books and online tutorials.

Giga OM

Giga OM, a network of technology websites published by Om Malik and his team, is one of the leading sources of information about the information technology industry.

CNet

One of the first technology news websites, CNet writes about the technology industry in depth. CNet is also a great place to visit for product reviews.

Read Write Web

RWW covers the next generation of websites and web services, with an emphasis on "Web 2.0" services and companies.

Venture Beat

Venture Beat, published by veteran reporter Matt Marshall and his team, covers high technology finance and product news. Venture Beat, along with O'Reilly and GigaOM, are required reading for news about the technology industry.
