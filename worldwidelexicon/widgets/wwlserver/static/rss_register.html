RSS Translation Service

The Worldwide Lexicon, in partnership with SpeakLike is now offering a turnkey translation service for RSS news feeds. Simply register your website or blog's RSS feed with us, and we will automatically translate new articles as you publish them. You can select any combination of machine translation or professional translation. To enable professional translation, simply provide your SpeakLike credentials. SpeakLike charges between 5 to 10 cents per word depending on the language selected and generally submits translations within an hour of publication.
