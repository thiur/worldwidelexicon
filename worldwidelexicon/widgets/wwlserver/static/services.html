Professional Services

Worldwide Lexicon Inc provides a variety of professional services to organizations. We publish our software, free of charge, under an open source license. If you are an experienced web developer, and want to build your own system, you can download the source code and modify it however you need to.

We also offer consulting and management services to clients, to assist you in designing, building and maintaining multilingual services. Among the services we offer are:

  • Turnkey systems. We build and manage your translation system, so you can focus your attention on your business.
  • Custom software development. We can build custom software, to adapt the system to your needs. For example, you can hire us to build a module to connect WWL to your website, publishing system, etc.
  • Business analysis and project management. We can help you understand where you can build an international audience, what languages to support, and how to expand into new markets. We can help develop a product roadmap and development plan that fits within your budget and schedule.
  • Website localization. Our new on-demand professional translation service enables you to translate your website or web application, using professional translators.
  • Translation portals. If you are a publisher, we can help you build a translation portal where your readers can go to view, create and edit translations for pages on your website.
  • Translation communities. If you have a large and active user community, you can build an online community that is focused on translation.
